# Campaign: Stoneforge Guild

## Central conflict
A lone rogue named Donovan Ruby has joined the Stoneforge Guild and assembled
a band of three companions for an escort quest along the Old Trade Road
between Stoneforge and the market town of Redhaven. The road passes through
Stonebridge, Hollowmoor, and the Whispering Woods.

## Major hooks
- Escort the merchant caravan from Stonebridge to Redhaven by dawn
- Investigate the goblin-ravaged farmstead on the road
- Survey the lost mine near the Crystal Caves

## Primary NPCs
### Eldrin Stormbow (ranger, Stonebridge)
A half-elf ranger of the Stoneforge Guild. Calloused hands of a seasoned
traveler. Eyes that scan the brush ahead constantly. Once tracked a wounded
elk through the Whispering Woods to a hidden druidic circle. Scouts ahead of
the party on the Old Trade Road.

### Lira Songheart (bard, Stonebridge)
A human bard who travels with a lute slung across her shoulder. Defused a
tavern brawl with a quick-thinking ballad once. Light tunes drift on the
wind when she's near. Joined the band for the road-side adventure of it.

### Borin Ironhand (cleric, Stonebridge)
A stout dwarf cleric. Mutters low prayers under his breath before any
journey. Once helped rebuild a shattered altar at the Forge-Temple after a
goblin raid. Carries an axe at his side and a steady mug in his hand.

## Key locations
### Stoneforge Guild Hall (guild hall)
Lantern-lit common room smelling of iron from the smithy beyond. A sturdy
oak notice board carries quest postings — Escort the Merchant Caravan,
Lost Mine Survey, Goblin-Ravaged Farmstead. Where the party assembled.

### Old Trade Road (road)
The dirt road east from Stoneforge through the rolling hills toward
Redhaven. Passes a stone cairn at a fork into the woods, climbs a low
ridge, drops into the valley toward Stonebridge.

### Stonebridge (town)
A market town spanning the River Wynd via a great stone arch. Slate roofs
catch the evening sun. Lanterns swing from timber posts over the
cobblestones. The Stumbling Stag inn sits near the square, painted with a
crooked-antlered stag. Where the merchant caravan departs at dawn.

### The Stumbling Stag (tavern in Stonebridge)
A timber inn with a swinging sign of a crooked-antlered stag, illuminated
by amber lanterns. Heavy oak door. Spiced ale and roasting pork. A burly
dwarf wipes mugs behind the bar. Hearth-lit common room.

### Redhaven (town)
A coastal market town to the east, the caravan's destination. Salt-stained
docks. The Iron Tankard's lantern glows at the far end of the docks.

### Hollowmoor (village)
A misty thatched-roof village reached over a worn stone bridge. Low
moss-clad hills. A stone fountain in the village square. A timber tavern
with a swinging goat sign. A blacksmith at his forge.

### Whispering Woods (forest)
Dark woods that stretch beside the Old Trade Road, leaves trembling
without wind. A hidden druidic circle is rumored to lie within.

## Factions
### Stoneforge Guild (adventuring guild)
The guild that posts quests on the notice board. Donovan Ruby and the
companions are members. Based in the Stoneforge Guild Hall.
